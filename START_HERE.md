# Start Here: A 10-Minute AI Workflow Setup

Use this page when you only have a few minutes and want a safe starting point.

## 1. Choose one task

Pick one:

- Understand a difficult concept.
- Find five candidate papers.
- Read across several source documents.
- Improve a draft from verified notes.
- Analyze a small non-sensitive dataset.

## 3. Use a safe prompt

Copy a prompt from `prompts/` and replace the bracketed text.

Do not ask the AI to write your final submission. Ask it to explain, structure, compare, critique, or check.

## 5. A good first experiment

Try this:

> I am learning [topic]. Explain it at two levels: first for a beginner, then for a postgraduate management student. Ask me three Socratic questions to test my understanding. Then identify two common misunderstandings.

Your goal is not to get a perfect answer. Your goal is to build a better learning loop.
