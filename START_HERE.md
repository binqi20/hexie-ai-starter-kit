# Start Here: A 10-Minute AI Workflow Setup

Use this page when you want a safe starting point.

## 1. Choose one task

Pick one:

- Understand a difficult concept.
- Find five candidate papers.
- Build a source pack.
- Turn one source into a research note.
- Improve a draft from verified notes.
- Analyze a small non-sensitive dataset.

## 3. Use the v2 research loop

For research or evidence-heavy tasks, use:

```text
source pack -> research note -> evidence anchors -> independent audit -> writing/action
```

Minimum files:

- `templates/source-pack-manifest.md`
- `templates/management-research-note.md`
- `templates/evidence-anchor-table.md`
- `templates/audit-report.md`

## 4. Copy a safe prompt

Useful first prompts:

- `prompts/socratic-tutor.md`
- `prompts/literature-search.md`
- `prompts/research-note-extraction.md`
- `prompts/independent-source-audit.md`
- `prompts/literature-synthesis-from-notes.md`

Do not ask AI to write your final submission from nothing. Ask it to explain, structure, compare, critique, extract, or check.

## 6. A good first experiment

Try this:

```text
Read docs/student-research-workflow.md. Help me create a source-pack manifest for a five-paper sprint on [topic]. Do not invent sources. Mark every source status clearly and tell me what I must verify manually.
```

Your goal is not to get a perfect answer. Your goal is to build a better learning and research loop.
