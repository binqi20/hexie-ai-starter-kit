# Prompt: Socratic Tutor

Copy and adapt:

```text
I am a postgraduate student learning [topic].

Please act as a Socratic tutor.

1. Explain the topic at two levels:
   - beginner level
   - postgraduate management level
2. Give one example related to [GIS / SHR / SAH / my field].
3. Ask me three Socratic questions.
4. After I answer, identify misconceptions and correct them.
5. Separate confirmed knowledge from interpretation.
```
