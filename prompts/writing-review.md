# Prompt: Writing Review With Evidence

Copy and adapt:

```text
I will provide a draft and my source notes.

Please review the draft for:
1. clarity of argument
2. paragraph structure
3. unsupported claims
4. missing counterarguments
5. citation risks
6. places where the wording overclaims the evidence

Do not rewrite the whole draft unless I ask. Give targeted revision suggestions.

Use this format:
- Strongest part
- Highest-risk claim
- Missing evidence
- Revision suggestions
- Verification checklist
```
