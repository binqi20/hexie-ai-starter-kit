# 03. Grounded Reading

Grounded reading means the AI answers from a source pack you provide, not from vague memory.

## Best use cases

- Compare several readings.
- Extract key definitions.
- Find agreement and disagreement across sources.
- Prepare a discussion note.

## Workflow

1. Upload or provide the source documents.
2. Ask the AI to answer only from those sources.
3. Request citations or source locations for every important claim.
4. Click or open the cited passage.
5. Create your own synthesis.

## Good questions

- Where do these sources agree?
- Where do they disagree?
- What assumption does each source make?
- What evidence is strongest?
- What remains uncertain?

## Verification habit

Click every citation you plan to use. If you cannot find the claim in the source, do not use it.

## HeXie link

The workflow creates discipline around evidence. The human still interprets what the evidence means.
