# 01. Learning Workflows

Use AI as a tutor, not as a shortcut.

## Best use cases

- Understand a theory, concept, or method.
- Compare similar concepts.
- Prepare for a seminar discussion.
- Test your own understanding.

## Workflow

1. State the concept and your current level.
2. Ask for a two-level explanation.
3. Ask for examples from your programme or pathway.
4. Ask the AI to question you.
5. Answer in your own words.
6. Ask for correction and misconception checks.

## Recommended prompt

Use `prompts/socratic-tutor.md`.

## Verification habit

Ask:

- Which part is explanation?
- Which part is interpretation?
- Which part needs a textbook, paper, or instructor confirmation?

## HeXie link

The AI provides structure and examples. You provide meaning, judgment, and final understanding.
