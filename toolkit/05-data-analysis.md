# 05. Data Analysis

Use AI for small, non-sensitive datasets when you can verify the logic.

## Best use cases

- Clean a CSV.
- Generate descriptive statistics.
- Produce simple charts.
- Explore survey patterns.
- Check assumptions before deeper analysis.

## Do not upload

- Employee-identifiable data.
- Patient or health records.
- Interview transcripts without permission.
- Confidential company data.
- Data covered by ethics or legal restrictions.

## Workflow

1. Confirm the data is safe to upload.
2. Describe each column.
3. Ask for a data-quality check.
4. Ask for descriptive statistics.
5. Ask for one or two charts.
6. Ask for assumptions and limitations.
7. Check the calculations and labels.

## Verification habit

Check:

- Row count.
- Column mapping.
- Missing values.
- Group definitions.
- Chart labels.
- Whether the conclusion exceeds the data.
