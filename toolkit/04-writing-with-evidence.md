# 04. Writing With Evidence

Use AI after you have sources, notes, and an argument. Do not start by asking for a full essay.

## Best use cases

- Turn notes into an outline.
- Improve clarity and structure.
- Test whether an argument is coherent.
- Identify missing evidence.
- Prepare counterarguments.

## Workflow

1. Provide your research question.
2. Provide verified notes or source excerpts.
3. Ask for an outline, not a final essay.
4. Ask what evidence is missing.
5. Draft in your own voice.
6. Ask the AI to review logic, clarity, and evidence use.
7. Check all citations manually.

## Safer AI tasks

- "Compare these two outline options."
- "Find unsupported claims in this paragraph."
- "Suggest clearer topic sentences."
- "Identify where I need stronger evidence."

## Verification habit

For every paragraph, ask: what claim is made, what source supports it, and have I checked the source?
