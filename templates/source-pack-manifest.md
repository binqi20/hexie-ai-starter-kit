## Source status rules

- `opened_full_text`: you opened the original source.
- `metadata_only`: you have only bibliographic data or abstract.
- `secondary_summary`: you have a summary about the source, not the source itself.
- `not_checked`: you have not verified it.

Do not use `metadata_only`, `secondary_summary`, or `not_checked` as final evidence.
